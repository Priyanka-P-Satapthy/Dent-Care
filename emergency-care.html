What constitutes a dental emergency?

Dental emergencies include severe toothaches, broken or knocked-out teeth, severe bleeding, abscesses, and injuries to the mouth. If you're experiencing severe pain or have sustained an injury to your mouth, please contact us immediately.

How quickly can I get emergency care?

We aim to provide emergency care within 30 minutes of your call. Our emergency response team is available 24/7, and we prioritize urgent cases to ensure you receive immediate attention and relief.

Do you accept insurance for emergency care?

Yes, we accept most dental insurance plans for emergency care. We also offer flexible payment options and can work with you to ensure you receive the care you need when you need it most.
